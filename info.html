■ Who We Are

Identity Customs

Identity Customs is Louisville's home for screen printing, graffiti art, and custom goods. We operate at the intersection of street culture and craft — bringing the raw energy of the wall into wearable, printable, tangible form.

We're not a corporate print shop. We're a crew of artists, writers, and makers who care deeply about the work — every piece that leaves our hands carries identity.

■ The Culture

Rooted in the Streets

Graffiti isn't decoration — it's communication. It's the oldest form of public identity, the original custom print. Identity Customs takes that lineage seriously, bringing the discipline, craft, and intention of graffiti writing into every project we touch.

Louisville has a deep creative underground and we're proud to be part of it. From the walls to the racks, we rep the city and the culture that built us.
